Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. South Carolina. The Angel Oak — a massive ancient live oak with enormous sprawling branches draped in resurrection fern and Spanish moss. Pristine lowcountry salt marshes stretch to the horizon behind it at golden hour. Warm, humid, atmospheric southern light. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
