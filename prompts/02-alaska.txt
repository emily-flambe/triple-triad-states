Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Alaska. The massive snow-covered peak of Denali dominates the sky above a vast glacial valley. Tidewater glaciers calve into a turquoise fjord below. Northern lights shimmer green and purple across the twilight sky. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
