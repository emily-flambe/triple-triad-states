Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Minnesota. The Boundary Waters' pristine lake reflects towering boreal forest of pine and birch under a vast dramatic sky. Northern lights dance green and purple overhead. Granite shoreline and still water. Cold, crystalline northern atmosphere. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
