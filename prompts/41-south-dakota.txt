Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. South Dakota. The Badlands' dramatic eroded spires and pinnacles in bands of pink, gray, and orange stretch across a vast landscape. Dark Ponderosa pine forest of the Black Hills rises in the background. Dramatic western sky with storm light. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
