Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Arkansas. The Buffalo River flows through a narrow valley beneath towering Ozark limestone bluffs draped in autumn forest. Morning mist rises from the water. Warm light filters through the hardwood canopy. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
