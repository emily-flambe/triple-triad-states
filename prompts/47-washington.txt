Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Washington. Mount Rainier's massive glaciated peak rises above a sea of clouds. The Hoh Rainforest's moss-draped old-growth Sitka spruce tower cathedral-like in the foreground. Olympic coast sea stacks rise from misty Pacific surf. Moody, powerful, atmospheric Pacific Northwest light. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
