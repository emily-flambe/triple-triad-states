Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Wisconsin. Apostle Islands' sea caves with ice formations along Lake Superior's shoreline. Dense maple and birch forest blazes with autumn gold on the clifftops above. Cold, crystalline Great Lakes light. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
