Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Idaho. Hells Canyon — the deepest river gorge in North America — plunges dramatically with the Snake River far below. The jagged granite Sawtooth Mountains rise in the distance. Dense old-growth cedar and pine forest blankets the steep canyon walls. Shoshone Falls cascades in the midground. Wild, rugged, remote atmosphere. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
