Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Missouri. The Current River flows crystal-clear through towering Ozark dolomite bluffs covered in autumn forest. Morning mist rises from the spring-fed water. Dense hardwood canopy in warm golden light. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
