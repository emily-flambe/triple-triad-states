Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. New York. The thundering cascade of Niagara Falls dominates the scene with rainbow mist rising into dramatic clouds. The Hudson River Valley stretches into the distance with autumn-colored Catskill Mountains beyond. Cold, powerful, grand atmosphere. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
