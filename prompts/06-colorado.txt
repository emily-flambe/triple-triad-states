Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Colorado Rocky Mountains. The iconic Maroon Bells twin peaks behind a mirror-still alpine lake. Red sandstone spires of Garden of the Gods rise in the foreground. Golden aspen groves blanket the valley. Ethereal golden light breaks through dramatic storm clouds, casting god-rays across the peaks. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
