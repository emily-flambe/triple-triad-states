Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Oregon. Crater Lake's impossibly blue caldera fills an ancient volcanic rim. Old-growth temperate rainforest with massive ferns and moss-draped trees frames the shore. Cannon Beach's Haystack Rock rises from misty Pacific surf in the far distance. Moody, atmospheric Pacific Northwest light. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text.
