Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Connecticut. A rocky New England shoreline with autumn-colored hardwood forest of blazing maples and oaks covering rolling hills down to Long Island Sound. Morning fog rolls across the water. Warm golden fall light. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
