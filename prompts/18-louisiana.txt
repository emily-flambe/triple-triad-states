Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Louisiana. Ancient bald cypress trees draped in Spanish moss rise from mirror-still bayou water reflecting a blood-red sunset. Mist curls between the trees. The Atchafalaya Basin swamp stretches into the distance. Deep, mysterious southern gothic atmosphere. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
