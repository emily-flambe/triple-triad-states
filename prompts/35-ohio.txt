Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Ohio. The winding Cuyahoga River cuts through a lush forested valley in autumn color. Lake Erie's vast freshwater horizon stretches in the distance. Rolling green farmland and gentle Appalachian foothills surround the river. Warm golden Midwestern light. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text.
