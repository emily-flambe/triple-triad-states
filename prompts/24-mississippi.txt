Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Mississippi. The mighty Mississippi River curves wide through vast bottomland cypress swamp. Spanish moss hangs heavy from ancient trees reflected in mirror-still black water. Morning mist rises from the river. Deep golden southern light filters through the haze. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
