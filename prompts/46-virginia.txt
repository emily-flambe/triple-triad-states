Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Virginia. The Blue Ridge Mountains fade in layered ridges along Skyline Drive. Natural Bridge's massive limestone arch spans a forested gorge in the foreground. Shenandoah Valley's patchwork of farmland and forest stretches below. Warm, golden, atmospheric Appalachian light. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
