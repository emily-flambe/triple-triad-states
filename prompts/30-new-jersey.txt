Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. New Jersey. The Pine Barrens' mysterious ancient pygmy pine forest surrounds dark tea-colored cedar swamp water. Sandy barrier island dunes visible in the distance where they meet the Atlantic. Misty, atmospheric golden coastal light. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
