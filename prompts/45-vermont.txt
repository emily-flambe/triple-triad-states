A covered bridge spans a rocky mountain stream in the foreground. Rolling pastoral farmland with morning mist in the valley. Quintessential New England autumn light. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text.
