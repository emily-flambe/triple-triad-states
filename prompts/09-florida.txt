Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Florida. A sweeping white sand beach with turquoise Gulf waters and dramatic cumulus clouds towering overhead. Sea oats sway in the foreground. Palm trees line the shore. A dramatic tropical sunset with deep oranges and purples reflects off the wet sand. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
