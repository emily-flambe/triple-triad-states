Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Pennsylvania. The wide Susquehanna River winds through rolling Pocono Mountains covered in blazing autumn hardwood forest. Weathered red barns and stone farmhouses dot the pastoral valley below. Morning mist fills the hollows between forested Appalachian ridges. Rich golden Pennsylvania autumn light. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
