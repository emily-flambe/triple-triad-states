Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. Georgia. A grand avenue of ancient live oaks draped in Spanish moss leads toward the misty Georgia coast. Okefenokee Swamp's mirror-black water and cypress knees visible to one side. Warm humid southern golden hour light. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
