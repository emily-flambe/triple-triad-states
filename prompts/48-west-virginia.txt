Fantasy landscape oil painting for a Magic: The Gathering land card. West Virginia. The New River Gorge Bridge spans a vast forested canyon. Seneca Rocks' dramatic sandstone fins rise vertically from the forest below. Dense Appalachian hardwood forest covers endless mountain ridges in autumn color. Misty, ancient, wild atmosphere. Epic scale, luminous atmosphere, rich saturated oil paint textures. Style of John Avon and Noah Bradley MTG land art. No text. The artwork must fill the entire canvas edge to edge with no margins, no padding, no white space, no borders.
